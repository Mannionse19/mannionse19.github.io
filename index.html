This year I recieved three varisty letters for competing in Cross Country, Basketball, and Crew. Joining Cross Country last second after much encouragement from Mr. Richards, Mr. George, and teamates from last spring. The Cross Country Team won ISL's wich is the only reason that I recieved a letter as a "contributer". The attitude shift from Cross Country to Varisty Basketball cannot be understated. Although I played not as frequently as I would have liked, the expirence I gained from playing with skilled individuals and competitors like Daivd and Jovan helped prepare me for the spring season. Going into Crew season I was not expecting much because of the critical decision I had made earlier in the year to play basketball rather than winter training. No one could have predicted the improvments I made and by the first race I was sitting in the bow of the first boat. From last year when I was one second from the hall of fame (4:50 1500m) I dropped over ten seconds to pull on the last erg test of this year a 4:39.6. Sean this is proof to yourself that effort brings results and you need to use this for encouragement over the summer and into next year. Next years athletic acheivements are your future set the bar high.
